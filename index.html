Business Analyst Intership - Technical case
Netflix case.

March 10, 2022

Congratulations, today you have been hired as a business analyst at Netflix@database ! As your first mission, you were asked to perform an exploratory analysis of the content available on Netflix. You will ultimately have to present your analysis to someone from the company that tries to understand what type of content is available on the platform worldwide. More specifically, he asked you to answer these 5 questions:

What has been the growth of content over the years by type of show ?
Is there seasonality in the dates when titles are added on the platform?
What are the top 5 movie directors that have the longest total movie duration available on Netflix ?
Any insights/recommendations you would have for the company in terms of growth strategy?
Bonus: What are the top 5 actors seen on Netflix in the United States since 2018 (year of release) (those with the most titles)

  • Full Script

What has been the growth of content over the years
by type of show ?

- Between 2008 and 2021 the number of Tv Shows went from 1 to 2400.
- Between 2008 and 2021 the number of Movies went from 1 to 5377.
- Conclusion: There has been a huge increase of Movies and Tv Shows over the years but Movies have grown faster than Tv shows.

Is there seasonality in the dates
when titles are added on the platform?

- We can see that there is a seasonailty trend. Indeed, we see that the trimester 4 is over represented with regards to the other trimesters who have similar results.
- Conclusion: Titles must be added in the fourth trimester.

What are the top 5 movie directors that
have the longest total movie duration available on Netflix ?

The first place is dedicated to Martin Scorsese, the second to Cathy Garcia Molina, the third to Youssef Chahine, the fourth to Steven Spielberg and the last one to David Dhaawan.

Any insights/recommendations you would have for the company
in terms of growth strategy?

- Improve the production of movies since it is the main content and the most liked.
- Add the content during the fourth trimester (October to December) as content are more likely to be viewed.
- Try to work in priority with Martin Scorsese, Cathy Garcia Molina, Youssef Chahine, Steven Spielberg, David Dhaawan because their movies are very popular and they are a safe bet.
